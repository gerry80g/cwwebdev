The European Computer Driving Licence is an SCQF level 5 course made up of 7 units. All 7 units must be completed to achieve your certificate.

The units are mainly practical - from using office applications, managing files and using the internet and email applications. There are also a couple of units which require you to demonstrate knowledge and understanding of IT security and the fundamentals of using the operating system.

  • Unit 1 - IT Security
  • Unit 2 - IT User Fundamentals
  • Unit 3 - Word Processing
  • Unit 4 - Spreadsheets
  • Unit 5 - Databases
  • Unit 6 - Presentations
  • Unit 7 - Internet & Email
